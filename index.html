Welcome to Duel.

The game and its engine are 100% open source.

Connect two or more controllers for local multiplayer.

Controls

Mouse & Keyboard

W,A,S,D - Movement

Q, E - Switch weapons

Click to Shoot

Space - Shield (for multiplayer)

Gamepads (XBOX360 Controllers)

Left Thumb Stick / Up, Down, Left, Right - Movement

Right Thumb Stick - Aim

RB, LB - Switch weapons

RT - Shoot

LT - Shield (for multiplayer)
